Smart Travel Tips for Exploring India

From packing right to budgeting smartly, ensure a safe and hassle-free journey.

Packing Essentials

  • Carry light cotton clothes for summer, warm layers for winters, and rain gear for monsoons.
  • Wear comfortable footwear, sturdy shoes for sightseeing, and sandals for temples.
  • Bring sunscreen, insect repellent, power banks, and universal travel adapters.

Budget Travel Tips

  • Use Indian Railways, metro services, and government buses for affordable transport.
  • Book accommodations and transport in advance for better deals.
  • Enjoy local street food for an authentic yet budget-friendly dining experience.

Safety Tips

  • Be cautious of scams and unauthorized guides or overpriced transport services.
  • Use anti-theft backpacks, avoid carrying excessive cash, and keep valuables secure.
  • Save emergency contacts, including police, ambulance, and embassy numbers.

Cultural Etiquette

  • Dress modestly in religious places and remove footwear before entering temples.
  • Greet people with "Namaste" by folding hands instead of shaking hands.
  • Seek permission before taking photos of locals and respect their privacy.
