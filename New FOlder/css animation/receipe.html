Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar, Water, Milk Ingredients, Enriched Wheat Flour
  • Canola Oil, Palm Oil, Vegetable Shortening, Semolina
  • Sodium Aluminum Phosphate, Sodium Bicarbonate
  • Natural Rose Water Flavour

Receipe

  1. Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee, and milk.
  2. Balls: Roll into smooth balls.
  3. Syrup: Boil sugar, water, and cardamom.
  4. Fry: Deep-fry until golden.
  5. Soak: Soak in warm syrup.
